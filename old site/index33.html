Features

Our host of features are designed to allow charities to upload and manage stock to online marketplaces with as few processes as possible.

Detect Product Attributes

Using machine learning algorithms Doneight application is able to detect product attributes from a series of images of the product. The custom trained machine learning model is able to identify products such as clothing, books, and household appliances.

Push to Online Marketplaces

Once product images and product copy is ready and approved the items are uploaded to online marketplaces that are relevant for the product. For items such as arts and craft, they will be pushed to platforms such as Etsy and clothing to platforms such as eBay.

Manage Stock Inventories

All the stock uploaded to platforms will be managed centrally from doneight's web app and mobile application. When items are ordered stock levels are automatically updated across all platforms.

Order Notifications

When new orders come in you will be nootified via email and app notifications. Shipping labels and trracking information is attached for ease of use.
